Case Study

Nexus Platform

A complete digital ecosystem for the next generation of enterprise collaboration.

Client Nexus Corp
Year 2024
Services Strategy, Design, Development
Industry Enterprise SaaS
( The Challenge )

An outdated platform holding back a growing enterprise

Nexus Corp had built a powerful collaboration tool, but years of incremental updates left them with a fragmented user experience. Teams were abandoning the platform for simpler alternatives, engagement was declining quarter over quarter, and the technical debt made every new feature a months-long ordeal.

They needed more than a redesign. They needed a complete rethink of how their product looked, felt, and performed — without disrupting the workflows their most loyal users depended on.

( The Solution )

A ground-up rebuild with strategy at its core

We started with a four-week discovery phase: stakeholder interviews, user research sessions, and a full audit of the existing platform. This gave us a clear map of what to keep, what to kill, and what to build from scratch.

The new design system unified the entire product under a single, coherent visual language — scalable components, consistent spacing, and an accessibility-first approach. We introduced a modular dashboard that lets teams customize their workspace, a real-time notification engine, and a completely rearchitected navigation that reduced time-to-task by over 60%.

On the engineering side, we migrated from a legacy monolith to a modern Next.js architecture with server-side rendering, edge caching, and a headless CMS for their marketing pages. The result: page loads under 1 second, a Lighthouse score of 98, and a codebase their team could actually maintain.

( The Results )

Numbers that speak for themselves

Within three months of launch, Nexus saw a dramatic shift across every key metric. User engagement surged, page performance hit best-in-class benchmarks, and their product team was shipping features in days instead of months.

+40% User Engagement
2.5x Page Views
